Accessibility

The University of Georgia follows the Section 508 Standards for web accessibility.  Please contact your instructor with accessibility problems in this course.

If you have a disability and would like to request specific accommodations, please contact your instructor.  If you plan to request accommodations for a disability, please register with the UGA Disability Resource Center, 114 Clark Howell Hall, Athens, GA 30602. Phone: 706.542.8719 Fax: 706.542.7719 Email: dsinfo@uga.edu
