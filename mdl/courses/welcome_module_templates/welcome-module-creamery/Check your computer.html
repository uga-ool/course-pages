Check Your Computer

Minimum technical requirements

  • Access to high-speed internet (cable or DSL)
  • A computer with: 1GB RAM Minimum; 2GB+ preferred
  • 20 GB free disk space
  • Sound card, microphone, and speakers
  • Monitor capable of 1024x768 display
  • Operating System: Windows Vista Service Pack 2, Windows 7, Windows 8; Mac OS X 10.7 or higher
  • Word processing software
  • Access to a printer (suggested)
